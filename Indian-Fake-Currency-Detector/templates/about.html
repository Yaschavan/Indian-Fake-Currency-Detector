Project context

Why counterfeit detection needs a simple public-facing interface

This project is aimed at making note verification more approachable for people who do not have access to bank-grade screening systems.

What this app is trying to solve

Counterfeit currency affects everyday cash transactions, especially in situations where people cannot rely on digital payments. While banks and commercial systems often have dedicated hardware for verification, most individuals do not.

This detector creates a simpler software-based workflow: upload the note image, choose the denomination, and view a confidence-backed result in a format that is easy to understand.

Problem

Fake currency creates trust issues in markets where cash still plays a major role.

Approach

Image-based analysis is used to compare uploaded notes and classify them as genuine or fake.

Goal

Present the output in a way that feels understandable, fast, and usable for common people.
